About

Hi ! I am Nguyen Nguyen, usually go by Trung Nguyen. I was born and raised in Vietnam, inheriting a purely traditional education, which sometimes bombarded me with worn-out conventions. Paired with countless debates I competed professionally when I was in highschool, these somehow shaped my lens to the world. At 17, I got a scholarship from a Vietnamese university and decided to take a solo travel to 17 countries. After that, I decided to study abroad, and now currently enrolling at Minerva University with a full-tuition scholarship. I am now interested in learning how to code, to research, to write professionally, and to spend quality time with my family and friends. My dream is to have a garden on the Swiss Alps, curating artworks, with my best people.
